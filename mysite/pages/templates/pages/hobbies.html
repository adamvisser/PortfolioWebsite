Adam Visser's Hobbies

There is a very good chance I spend too much of my free time doing tech things...

As it stands right now trying to get an idea to change the world going. Changing the world drives me to do a lot of what I do, even if the world being changed is just one persons world. I always like to say I will program anything if it makes a difference.

When I am not trying to change the world there is a really good chance that I am playing with something that has wheels. In many cases when I have free time I will either build out one of the multitude of electric skateboards I have, or I will be working on my jeep.

My electric skateboard has some really large wheels, gears, pulleys, and a lot of random RC car/airplane parts all slapped on it. This combination of parts on a longboard allows me to travel from A, to the bart, to B. More often than not its how I get to work, and how I get into the most trouble.

When I am not using electricity to power me around I have a crazy old jeep that I am trying to "restomod". My hope is that since it is so old, and everything needs to be replaced, it is a perfect starting point to make my own form of jeep. It also has a very large American V8 in it, so thats great for burning some dead dinosaurs.
